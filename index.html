About me

Yashuai Cao received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in communication engineering from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT) and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, in 2017 and 2022, respectively. He is currently a lecturer in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), Baoding. His current research interests include wireless resource allocation, signal processing for massive MIMO systems, intelligent reflecting surface, physical layer security, cell-free communications, and wireless federated learning.
