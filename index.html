1. Biography

Yashuai Cao received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in communication engineering from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT) and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, in 2017 and 2022, respectively. From 2022 to 2023, he was a lecturer in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), Baoding. He is currently a postdoctoral research fellow with the Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China.


My research interests involves several fields of Wireless Communications, including Massive MIMO, Intelligent Reflecting Surface, Integrated Sensing And Communications, Constructive Interference Precoding, and EM Wave-based Processing.
