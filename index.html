About Me

Yashuai Cao received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in communication engineering from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications (CQUPT) and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, in 2017 and 2022, respectively. From 2022 to 2023, he was a lecturer in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, North China Electric Power University (NCEPU), Baoding.


My research interests include:

  • Wireless resource allocation and signal processing for massive MIMO systems

  • Intelligent reflecting surface

  • Physical layer security

  • Cell-free communications

  • Wireless federated learning

Current Research Work

  • Integrated sensing and communications

  • Meta-surface enabled computing and related security and privacy issues
